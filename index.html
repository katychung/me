About Katy Chung

I am a UX designer from Scotland, previously in London and Hong Kong. I find myself frequently in Japan however, I am currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

I have a 1st class BSc in Interactive Media Design + 6 years of industry experience, largely with start-ups. When I'm not working, I'm bicycling, travelling photographing.

UX — People, Design, Technology

I mostly think about user experience design as shaping behaviour so I try to soak up knowledge on people; cognition, psychology, habits etc.

In the workplace, I value research, prototyping, user testing, continous improvement and collaboration (as opposed to "team work" which is merely working together). I easily immerse myself in all things technological; taking thing apart and re-assembling was a common occurrence growing up.

I have varying degrees of experience with user journeys, information architecture, aligning stakeholders, facilitation, metrics, JTBD, thinking hats, team critique, quantitative and qualitative research and so on and so fourth...
