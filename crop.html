Crop

UX Prototyping Product Management

Vyondは企業向けDIYビジネスアニメーション制作用SAASアプリ。短時間でアニメーション動画を作れます。

Vyond is a DIY animated video creation platform.

As part of a larger redesign (from Flash to HTML5), the "crop" feature underwent some necessary changes. This is no ordinary crop feature though, with many ambitious thoughts for its future, we needed to design and build with a lot of forethought and considerations.

We use the term "crop" to mean removing non-important aspects of an image. In Vyond, and in the video world, this means putting content into some sort of frame such as a television or behind a window.

As a team, we were very focused on making the interactions and UI consistent. There were many benefits to this:

      1) less mental load; no need to re-learn
      2) systematic design; team can focus on problems that matter)
      3) less code

This project is memorable for me because it enables our users to be even more creative with their animations. I enjoyed this project because it was a very technically challenging project that involved many teams including design, engineering, product and our internal animated video experts to help shape the product vision.
