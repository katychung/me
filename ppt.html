PowerPoint to Video

In the workplace, video has been growing in popularity as an alternative to PowerPoint. GoAnimate wanted to take users PowerPoint files and turn them into a animated video.

To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted and obfuscated confidential information in this case study. The information in this case study is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of GoAnimate / Vyond.

Problem

For a long time, presentations slides have been a workplace norm. Need to explain something to a group of people? PowerPoint probably comes to mind as a possible delivery medium. Animated video sounds daunting for most people, they conjure up making video as something that requires a lot of time and talent to create -- story, characters, structure, visual, audio, creativity! So how can we transition and show the value of animated video as an worthy alternative to the slides that they use so habitually?

PowerPoint to Video aimed to reduce the entry barrier to the Video Maker app by letting users bring in their existing PowerPoint files and converting them into an animated video.

Role

Initially the work was done with 2 engineers who focused on technical feasibility while I focused on the UX/UI. There was some direction from the Head of Product.

Goals

  • Setting user expectations about the resulting video.
  • Frequent feedback to user throughout the conversion process.
  • Speed - converting the PowerPoint file should not take more than a couple of mins.
  • Resulting video must be easy to edit

Reflection

  • Prototyping and testing would have been smarter and more cost effective.
  • The different ways decisions are made in a company have taught me a few lessons on management and leadership.
  • Timeboxing was very effective in delivering something on time but quality was affected. How could better project management allow for better quality..
